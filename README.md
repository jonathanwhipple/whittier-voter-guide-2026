# Whittier Voter Guide — April 14, 2026

An independent, nonpartisan voter guide for Whittier's April 14, 2026 municipal election.

**Three races. Eleven candidates. 33 Form 460 filings verified. 480 donors mapped. Every claim cited.**

## What's in this guide

- **Mayor** (3 candidates), **District 2** (5 candidates), **District 4** (3 candidates)
- Candidate backgrounds, platforms, campaign finance, and forum attendance
- Campaign finance verified directly from FPPC Form 460 filings on record with the Whittier City Clerk
- Donor geography (Whittier vs. outside) mapped by ZIP code from Schedule A entries
- Crime trends from the FBI Crime Data Explorer (Whittier PD, 2019-2025)
- Issue deep dives: Ficus Trees/Greenleaf Promenade, Crime, Homelessness, Immigration, Governance, Development
- Data visualizations: turnout trends, campaign finance comparisons, donor geography, crime trends, demographics
- Source marker system: ✓ (multi-source/public record), † (campaign-sourced), ‡ (single-source), ◊ (community-flagged)
- Candidate stance heat map, forum attendance grid, research toolkit

## Data sources

- **Campaign finance:** All 33 FPPC Form 460 PDFs filed with the Whittier City Clerk. 480 itemized donors extracted and classified by ZIP code.
- **Crime:** FBI Crime Data Explorer, Whittier PD (ORI: CA0197600)
- **Elections:** LA County Registrar-Recorder, Ballotpedia, City of Whittier
- **News:** Whittier Daily News, San Gabriel Valley Tribune, City of Whittier official records
- **Demographics:** U.S. Census Bureau
