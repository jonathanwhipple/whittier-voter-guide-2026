An independent, fact-checked voter guide for Whittier's April 14, 2026 municipal election.

**Three races. Eleven candidates. 50+ sources. Every claim cited.**

## What's in this guide

- **Mayor** (3 candidates), **District 2** (5 candidates), **District 4** (3 candidates)
- Candidate backgrounds, platforms, campaign finance, and forum attendance
- Issue deep dives: Ficus Trees/Greenleaf Promenade, Crime, Homelessness, Immigration, Governance, Development

- Full source marker system (✓ multi-source, † campaign-sourced, ‡ single-source)

- Research toolkit with links to every primary source database
